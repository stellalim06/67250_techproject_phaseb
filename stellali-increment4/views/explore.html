Mission Statement

MonoMuse exists at the crossroads of art and technology. Our mission is to create a space where visitors engage with digital innovation not as an abstraction, but as a living cultural force. We build exhibitions that challenge, inspire, and provoke conversation between people and the technologies that surround them.


Timeline

  • 2025: Museum expansion — new wing for digital installations
  • 2021: International museum collaboration
  • 2018: Museum opens in Pittsburgh, PA
